Intercept Form of a Line Equation

A line that does not go through the origin and is not parallel to the axes intersects them at two different points, (x0 , 0) and (0 , x0), with x0≠0 and y0≠0.

Such a line can be described with

xx0 + yy0 = 1

If you set x or y equal to zero, you can easily find the interception points

yy0 = 1 xx0 = 1
